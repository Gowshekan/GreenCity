About ECOSORT AI

Revolutionizing waste management through advanced artificial intelligence and computer vision

Our Mission

ECOSORT AI is dedicated to creating sustainable cities through cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions. Our advanced waste classification system helps individuals and communities properly sort waste, increasing recycling rates and reducing environmental pollution.

Technology

We use state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) with transfer learning, trained on thousands of waste images. Our AI model achieves high accuracy in classifying different types of waste materials, making recycling more accessible and efficient for everyone.

Impact

By automating waste sorting with AI, we contribute to SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities) and SDG 13 (Climate Action). Proper waste management reduces landfill usage, decreases pollution, and promotes circular economy principles for a sustainable future.
