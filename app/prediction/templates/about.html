About GreenCity

Revolutionizing waste management through artificial intelligence and computer vision

Our Mission

GreenCity is dedicated to creating sustainable cities through innovative AI solutions. Our waste classification system helps individuals and communities properly sort waste, increasing recycling rates and reducing environmental pollution.

Technology

We use state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) trained on thousands of waste images. Our model achieves high accuracy in classifying different types of waste materials, making recycling more accessible and efficient.

Impact

By automating waste sorting, we contribute to SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities) and SDG 13 (Climate Action). Proper waste management reduces landfill usage, decreases pollution, and promotes circular economy principles.
